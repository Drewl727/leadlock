Section 01

Why voicemail doesn't save you

Business owners treat voicemail as a safety net. It isn't. Of all callers who reach a voicemail box, 80% hang up without leaving a message. Of the 20% who do leave a message, most expect a callback within minutes — not hours.

The reality is that inbound phone calls are the highest-intent lead you'll ever get. Someone who picks up their phone and dials your number is ready to buy. They're not browsing. They're not comparing. They made a decision and they're acting on it. When you miss that call, you're not just missing a conversation — you're losing a customer who was already sold.

Hard truth

Only 37.8% of incoming calls to small businesses are answered by a live person. For every 10 calls you receive, roughly 6 reach nobody.

Section 02

The 5-minute window that decides everything

Speed to lead is everything in local service businesses. Research consistently shows that leads contacted within 5 minutes of their initial inquiry are 100x more likely to convert than leads contacted after 30 minutes. After one hour, conversion probability drops by 700%.

When a customer calls your auto shop and gets voicemail, they don't wait. They scroll back up on Google and call the next result. That entire process takes about 45 seconds. By the time you see the missed call notification and think to call back, the job is already booked — just not with you.

The window

You have roughly 5 minutes from the moment of a missed call to re-engage a lead before they commit to a competitor. No human can consistently hit that window. An AI can.

What AI text-back actually does

Missed call text-back is exactly what it sounds like. The moment a call goes unanswered, an automated text fires to the caller's number within seconds — not minutes. That text re-opens the conversation before they've had a chance to dial your competitor.

Here's how the sequence works in practice:

1

Call missed

Customer calls your shop. You're on another line, under a car, or it's after hours. Call goes unanswered.

2

Text fires in seconds

An automated, personalized text goes out immediately: "Hey, sorry we missed you! We'd love to help — what's going on with your vehicle?" Your number, your brand, your tone.

3

Lead responds

The customer replies via text. The AI handles the conversation — captures their info, qualifies the job, and either books them or routes the convo to you.

4

Job booked

You pick up a confirmed appointment on your calendar. The lead never called your competitor.

Recovery rate
