How Millrun Digital started

Millrun Digital started with a simple observation. Auto shops and small businesses across Hartford and Providence were leaking revenue every day — missed calls, review requests that never got sent, new customers who couldn't reach anyone after 5pm.

We fix that.

Why I built it this way

I run my own small businesses. I know what it feels like to miss a lead because you were busy doing the actual work. Millrun Digital is built the way it is because that's exactly the problem it needed to solve.
